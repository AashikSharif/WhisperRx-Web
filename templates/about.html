About WhisperRx

WhisperRx is an AI-powered medical scribing tool designed to assist doctors in creating structured, accurate, and efficient medical reports from audio conversations. Using the latest advancements in speech-to-text (via Whisper) and natural language generation (via Perplexity), WhisperRx transforms voice recordings into complete, structured medical notes in real time.

  • Voice-Based Input: Record consultations and let the AI transcribe and structure them.
  • Intelligent Summarization: Leveraging Perplexity, we generate SOAP-format medical summaries automatically.
  • Designed for Doctors & Patients: Two unique dashboards to streamline workflows for both sides.
  • Easy Access: Review past visits, view structured transcripts, and download reports easily.
  • Privacy & Security: All data is securely handled with role-based access control.
